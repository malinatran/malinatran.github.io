malina

Malina is a software developer based in Los Angeles. She designs and codes for the Internet, and is a believer in the infinite possibilities of technology to positively impact lives and society.

Currently, Malina works as a resident apprentice for 8th Light, a software consultancy. Previously, she was a developer at Sanctuary Computer and learned web development at General Assembly, where she was selected as an Opportunity Fund Fellow.

Malina strives to build community and make tech accessible to underrepresented communities. She has organized and hosted various hackathons and events, including GA Ladies and Queens Tech meetups. Through iMentor and ScriptEd, she has experience mentoring and teaching high school students learn how to code. Prior to coding, she has worked at the intersection of tech and urban planning, collaborating with government and stakeholders to foster tech ecosystems throughout New York City. ✌
