Hi, I'm Malina. I design and build things for the web through code. Currently, I am a student at General Assembly, participating in the Web Development Immersive program as an Opportunity Fund Fellow.

My previous work experience has been at the intersection of tech and urban planning. My roles have encompassed community engagement, policy, marketing, and partnerships. I have organized and hosted the inaugural Brooklyn Tech Triangle Mentorship Hackathon and Queens Tech Meetup series. I love going to hackathons and have participated in several of them as a coder, mentor, or judge.

I care about making cities better places to live, and creating opportunities for people from underrepresented communities. I'm a lifetime learner and a believer in the amazing possibilities of technology.
